The Eternal Garden

The Eternal Garden exists as a digital realm where artists become characters, music becomes narrative, and every interaction shapes the evolving story. This universe serves as both a creative platform and a living, breathing world that responds to the collective imagination of its inhabitants.

Within this realm, six independent artists have embarked on transformative journeys, each becoming a main character in the expansive storyline. Their music serves as the soundtrack to the CODEX, a mysterious book that holds the secrets of the garden.

The CODEX

The CODEX is your key to the Eternal Garden universe. While it carries many answers to the story of Eternal Garden, it leaves us with even more questions. The book has been engineered to bring the secrets of the garden to life with audio and video experiences.

In addition to fantastical visuals, the book also has an AR component; scan each page and experience animation, narrations, and original music that brings the story to life in ways never before possible.

The Artists' Journey

Each featured artist has embarked on a transformative journey to become a main character in the expansive storyline of Eternal Garden. This is the first RPG that musicians play using their creativity, blurring the lines between mediums to create an immersive and impactful storytelling experience.

Weaving the mediums of music, narration, imagery and animation together, while combining production tools with artificial intelligence, the artists create a dynamic and evolving stage setting journey for all who choose to enter the world of Eternal Garden.
